Task management for community projects.

Maybe you're organizing a local meetup, or an arts festival, or a movement for change, and you'd like to keep your board of directors on the same page.

Circle Blvd is for doing just that — it's for managing tasks, remembering who is doing what, and staying flexible as situations and priorities change.

Watch the walkthrough, take a tour.

Circle Blvd is a task management tool, made for teams of 3-12 people. Below is a 2-minute screencast on how to make a project and get started with the site.

This site supports:

  • Multiple projects
  • Unlimited accounts
  • Task lists, assignments, status updates, comments
  • Drag-and-drop prioritization
  • One-line task entry, multiple-task entry
  • Email notifications
  • Hashtags / label filtering
  • Reusable checklists
  • Roadmap planning
  • Task archives
  • Admin management
  • Secure communication over HTTPS

Visit our tour page to see a self-guided outline of the main aspects of the site.
